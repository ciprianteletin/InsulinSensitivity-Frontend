Input visualization and interpretation using self-explanatory charts

Your data from the OGTT test is analyzed and graphically represented using powerful charts. Based on your input, we can determine in what health category you are situated.

There are up to 12 category of diabetes, insulin resistance or pre-diabetes as follows:

  • Healthy, Impaired Fasting Glucose, Impaired Glucose Tolerance
  • Impaired Fasting Glucose + Impaired Glucose Tolerance, Diabetes
  • Diabetes with Impaired Glucose Tolerance, Diabetes with Impaired Fasting Glucose
  • Diabetes with Normal Fasting Glucose, Diabetes with Normal 2 hour Post-Glucose
  • Insulin Resistance derived from glucose at 30 or 60 minutes during OGTT test
